What is Edusify?

Edusify is a premium AI-powered productivity app designed for students preparing for NEET, JEE, Boards, and beyond. With AI tools for notes, flashcards, quizzes, and planning—Edusify helps students study smarter, not harder.

Features

  • 🧠 AI Assistant for instant answers & summaries
  • 📅 Smart Planner + AI To-Do Generator
  • 📝 One-tap Notes, Quizzes, Flashcards
  • 🎯 Personalized Leaderboards & Stats
  • 🔐 Document Locker & Sticky Notes
  • 🤝 Study Rooms & Friends
